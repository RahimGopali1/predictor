Model Improvements vs Standard Elo
Dixon-Coles Goal Correction
Adjusts 0-0, 1-0, 0-1, 1-1 scoreline probabilities with a ρ parameter to fix Poisson under-representation of draws.
Variable K-Factor
K=60 for World Cup matches, K=40 for continental tournaments, K=25 for qualifiers, K=10 for friendlies — mirroring FIFA's own weighting.
Dynamic In-Game State
A trailing team increases its attack rate by up to 25% in the final third of a match, reflecting real tactical shifts.
Nation Penalty Data
Each nation has a calibrated penalty conversion rate based on historical shootout data — Germany ~80%, England ~72%, etc.
Host Climate & Venue Modifier
Dallas (38°C) and Miami venues apply a heat penalty to high-pressing European teams. Vancouver benefits temperate sides.
95% Confidence Intervals
Championship probabilities shown with ±CI derived from simulation variance — not just point estimates.

50,000 Tournament Simulations

Run 50,000 tournament simulations to calculate championship odds.

Each simulation plays every group stage match, then resolves the full knockout bracket through to the final — including extra time and penalty shootouts. Elo ratings update dynamically within each simulation run, so a team that overperforms in the group stage enters the knockouts with a boosted rating. Results aggregate into precise championship odds with 95% confidence intervals.
