Team Ratings & Form

Elo ratings derived from 150 years of match history, updated with variable K-factors by match importance.

Like chess ratings, higher Elo = stronger team. Beating a top-10 side moves the needle far more than defeating a 50th-ranked nation. The K-factor scales by match type: World Cup final = K×60, friendly = K×10. Recent form modifiers use weighted decay — June 2026 matches count 100%, March matches 50%.

The Dixon-Coles model corrects standard Poisson for low-scoring outcomes. A ρ (rho) correlation term adjusts 0-0, 1-0, 0-1 and 1-1 probabilities, which Poisson systematically under or overestimates. Host teams (USA, Mexico, Canada) receive a +75 Elo bonus at home venues, with an additional climate modifier for hot-weather venues.
